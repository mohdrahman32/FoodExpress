Fun Facts About Desserts

  • Cheesecake dates back to ancient Greece and was even served to athletes during the first Olympic Games in 776 B.C.
  • In 1897, dentist William Morrison co-created this sugary treat, originally called "Fairy Floss."
  • The banana split was invented in 1904 by a 23-year-old pharmacist experimenting with dessert recipes.
  • The first gummy bear, "Gummibärchen," was created in 1922 by German confectioner Hans Riegel.
