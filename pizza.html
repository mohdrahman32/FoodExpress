Why Pizza is a Timeless Classic

Pizza is more than just a meal – it's a global symbol of comfort, indulgence, and joy. Originating from Italy, this dish has evolved into countless varieties, all catering to different tastes. From the simple Margherita to the hearty deep-dish pizza, there’s a slice for everyone.

Fun Facts About Pizza

  • Pizza is the most popular takeaway food worldwide.
  • The world’s largest pizza was created in Italy and was named “Ottavia.”
  • Each person in the U.S. eats an average of 46 pizza slices annually!
  • In 2017, the first-ever pizza-making robot was introduced in California.
