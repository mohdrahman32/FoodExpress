Fun Facts About Salads

  • The word "salad" comes from the Latin "sal," meaning salt.
  • Caesar salad was invented in Tijuana, Mexico, not Italy!
  • Salads have been enjoyed for over 2,000 years.
  • The largest salad ever made weighed over 44,000 pounds!
