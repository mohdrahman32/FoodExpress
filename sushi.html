Fun Facts About Sushi

  • The first sushi was invented in the 8th century as a way to preserve fish.
  • Sushi was originally eaten with fingers, not chopsticks.
  • The most expensive sushi roll in the world costs over $1,000.
  • Sushi rice is seasoned with vinegar, sugar, and salt to give it a distinctive taste.
